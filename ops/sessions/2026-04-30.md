## Picked (and why)
Shipped strikethrough stripping. The change is two parallel one-line
regex additions in `stripInlineMd` (notes) and `stripMd` (readme), plus
one test per detector. Yesterday's report named this as the next gap
and the precedent (single-underscore italic) made the shape obvious.
The `~~([^~\n]+)~~` regex can't match a `~~~` fence because the inner
class requires at least one non-tilde character, and fence detection
runs before the strip helper anyway, so the two don't collide.
